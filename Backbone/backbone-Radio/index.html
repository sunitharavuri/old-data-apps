The Radio does job ,that letting you perform actions across your application without needing to maintain references to otherwise unrelated views or models.

How Radio Works???

The Radio works by using the Backbone Events system you may be familiar with. You can fire an event on your Radio and any other objects listening to that event will pick it up and respond.

The Radio also provides an API for handling Request/Response patterns — essentially functions called without having direct access to them.

Radio Patterns

The Backbone Radio provides two major patterns: Events and Requests.
